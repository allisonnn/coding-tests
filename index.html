Accepted Risks

65 User Access
User access roles in the commission engine are not defined which leads to asccess by inappropriate personnel.
25 Compensation Plan
Compensation plans are not approved by appropriate level of management before being placed into production environment.
10 Commission Payment
Calculated commission payments are not paid in timely manner and are not properly accrued.
75 Inaccurate Sale Calculation
Sales are not completely captured by the commission engine.
